Havoc at the MTR


The mob had been obstructing trains in the morning rush hour with the most crippling taking place on 5 August 2019. They held up train doors, sounded emergency alarms, pretended to be passengers to feud with other commuters, and even pushed object onto the track. Their aim: stop you from going to work even if you dont join their strike. When they became disgruntled with the train operator after the later's failure to protect rioters against police, the mob went on to smashing up train stations and attacking passengers. These videos depicted the chaos caused by the mob, and how helpless genuine commuters were.
